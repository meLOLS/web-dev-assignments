Introduction

Story Of Humans

Human civilization is the story of how our ancestors transformed from small groups of hunter-gatherers into builders of cities, kingdoms, and modern nations. It is a journey marked by curiosity, cooperation, conflict, and the constant search for knowledge. Understanding civilization helps us understand ourselves

The Beginning

For most of human history, people lived by hunting animals and gathering plants. They travelled from place to place in search of food and water. Around ten thousand years ago, humans discovered agriculture, allowing them to settle permanently and build villages.
